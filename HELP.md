# Tetris Game Help

## How to Play

The goal of Tetris is to score as many points as possible by clearing horizontal lines of blocks.

## Controls

- **Left Arrow:** Move the falling tetromino to the left.
- **Right Arrow:** Move the falling tetromino to the right.
- **Down Arrow:** Move the falling tetromino down faster.
- **Up Arrow:** Rotate the falling tetromino.
- **'d' key:** Drop the tetromino to the bottom of the grid.

## Scoring

You score points by completing a horizontal line of blocks. When a line is completed, it will disappear, and the blocks above it will fall down.

## Game Over

The game is over when the blocks stack up to the top of the screen.
